Why build
on world

World Network already has over 22 million users, with approximately 380K people joining every week.

Each new user is onboarded through a single app —World App— and every World App user is fully onchain with a self-custodial wallet, free transaction fees, and a World ID. Combined, we have something brand new: a large, growing, fully onchain, verifiably human audience that is instantly accessible to builders.

What we're
looking for

We are looking for Mini Apps that provide utility, financial access and entertainment to World App users. Specifically, we believe the most interesting Mini Apps will take advantage of sybil resistance via Proof of Human and the integrated crypto wallet.

World builders finding the most success are the ones building from scratch for the context of Mini Apps. This is because Mini Apps have some unique characteristics:

  • 100% mobile audience reached via World App
  • Many users receiving the WLD airdrop and have funds ready to use
  • 100% of transaction fees covered
  • Users generally unfamiliar with crypto-native primitives & language
  • World Chain is part of the Optimism Superchain

Ultimately we want to support you in building what you are most excited about! For a specific list of ideas and categories we find compelling see our post with Variant here.

ABOUT WORLD

The World App is a user-friendly application designed to enable secure, decentralized transactions and interactions within the World ecosystem. It serves as a digital wallet and identity platform, integrating features like World ID for verifiable, privacy-preserving identity authentication and $WLD tokens for seamless transactions. With functionalities like free gas fees for verified users, the app supports decentralized finance (DeFi), peer-to-peer payments, and Mini App integration, aiming to make blockchain technology accessible to millions while promoting trust and inclusion.

ABOUT FWB

Friends With Benefits (FWB) is a global social DAO comprising over 5K artists, builders, and thinkers from the crypto and cultural sectors. Established in 2020, FWB leverages emerging technology tools to foster creative collaboration and community-driven initiatives. Members hold $FWB tokens, enabling collective funding, governance, and co-ownership of projects.
